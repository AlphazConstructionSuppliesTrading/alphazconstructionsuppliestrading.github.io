General Services

ALPHAZ GENERAL SERVICES, CLEANPRO SOLUTION is your trusted partner in comprehensive residential and commercial cleaning and maintenance. Strategically located in Wakas North, Pilar, Bataan, we deliver professional, safe, and high-quality cleaning solutions tailored to your specific needs. We specialize in a wide range of services—from regular home cleaning to complex post-construction, move-in/move-out, trauma cleaning, air conditioning maintenance, and solar panel cleaning for both residential and commercial properties.
